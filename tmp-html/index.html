The project

Library of Tamazight (Tamkaṛḍit n Tmaziɣt) is a website that gathers many scientific, artistic and cultural publications and websites on the Berber culture. Our goal is to help people to find a book to read or a website to visit in order to learn the culture, the languages and the history of Berber.
